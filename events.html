Events

Our events vary from year to year, but some traditional APASA events include:

APA Community Kickoff


The first-ever APA Community Kickoff took place on Sunday, September 14, 2014 at Harris Fisher Hall Museum. The beautiful venue held over 90+ APA community leaders within and beyond USC, providing a space for conversations, speeches, and performances. Delicious food from Food Fetish was served.


Tournament of Champions


The annual Tournament of Champions took place on Saturday, September 20, 2014 at McCarthy Quad. Thirteen APASA organizations were represented, and fought well. The trophy for 2014-2015 went to CASA! Congratulations!


Night Market


Night Market is arguably the biggest APASA event for the first semester. It is modeled after night markets you can traditionally find in Asia, mainly in Taiwain. At APASA’s Night Market, there were games and booths run by several APASA organizations. Food, boba, fluff ice, prizes, and musical performances decorated the night. Over 950 Trojans were able to enjoy the night.


Winter Formal


Another one of APASA’s signature fall events, APASA collaborated with six different student organizations on campus to bring a winter wonderland to campus this year! Four hours of dancing, along with scrumptious desserts, mocktails, a photo booth, a photo taking station, and a chocolate fountain graced the night. Nearly 600 students came and celebrated our own winter wonderland.


APAHF Extravaganza


Finally, our APA Heritage Festival was kicked off by our annual Extravaganza! There were 400 yummy tacos from Don Chow Tacos, and awesome performances from students. We also gave out some free swag and most importantly, stamp cards! These stamp cards are a new initiative for APAHF in which you get stamps per event you attend on the stamp card. You have the opportunity to get boba, various gift cards, or even Disneyland tickets! Check out our Facebook to find out more information!
